THE GUY WHO DIDN'T LIKE MUSICALS

Plot:

The Guy Who Didn’t Like Musicals is a horror-comedy musical.It follows Paul, an ordinary guy who doesn’t like musicals, and his friends and co-workers as the world around them suddenly transforms into one. What starts as a harmless song-and-dance quickly turns sinister, as the cheerful musical numbers are revealed to be part of an alien invasion taking over the minds of the townspeople.TGWDLM is part of the Hatchetfield series.
