BLACK FRIDAY

Plot:

Black Friday is a horror-comedy musical.It's Black Friday in the town of Hatchetfield, and this holiday season's hot ticket item is the brand new Tickle-Me Wiggly doll! He's cute. He's cuddly. He's evil. When supplies don't meet the demand, Tom Houston, Lex Foster, and the rest of the unfortunate souls at Lakeside Mall must fight their way through the shopping day from hell! When Wiggly hits the shelves, will the world survive... 'Black Friday?'
